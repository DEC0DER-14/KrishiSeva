Book Your Favorite Crops

Wheat is a staple grain used in making bread and cereals. It is widely grown across the Midwest region of the USA.

Corn is a major crop used for food, fodder, and biofuel. It thrives in warm climates, especially in Iowa and Nebraska.

Rice is the primary staple food for many countries. It's predominantly grown in Asia and parts of the Southern USA.

Soybeans are a versatile crop, often used for oil, food, and animal feed. It grows well in places like Illinois and Indiana.

Cotton is a key textile fiber grown across southern regions of the USA, like Texas and Mississippi.
